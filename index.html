The vision

NanoCell was built to handle database exports (or any large csv file) while staying lightweight and fast. It is cross-platform, distraction free, and keyboard driven to fit any work-flow (cheers to you mouse fearing developers 😉). An intuitive UI has also been added for a smooth learning curve. Last but not least, NanoCell presents powerful computing capabilities enabling you to work with variables and formulas.

NanoCell aims to provide a simple and free alternative to proprietary spreadsheet editors which are often too slow and cumbersome for the vast majority of their use-cases.
