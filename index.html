NanoCell is built for speed and simplicity.



It is perfect to quickly edit a few values and excells at handling very large files. It's view only mode enables you to easily jump in and out of database extracts to checkout their content. It does not try to interpret or compute cell values, keeping your file safe from any data-corruption.
NanoCell aims to provide a simple and free alternative to proprietary spreadsheet editors which are often too slow and cumbersome for the vast majority of their use-cases.
