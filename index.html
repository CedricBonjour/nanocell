NanoCell is built for speed and simplicity.



NanoCell lets you visualize data instantly, independent of the file size. It is an amazing tool to visualize large database extract on a moment's notice before moving on to your more powerful but slower frameworks. It is also perfect to edit smaller datasets such as config tables as it does not try to interpret or compute cell values, keeping your file safe from any data-corruption. When opening files that are not respecting csv format good practices, NanoCell will help you get your data back into shape.

NanoCell aims to provide a simple and free alternative to proprietary spreadsheet editors which are often too slow and cumbersome for the vast majority of their use-cases.
